Number theory studies the properties and relationships of integers.

Number is the ruler of forms and ideas, and the cause of gods and daemons. Pythagoras

The idea of "number" is one of the first things we learn, yet many of their properties are still a mystery. Modern number theory explores solutions to classic problems while also seeking deeper frameworks to explain the nature of numbers and their connections to geometric objects.
