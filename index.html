My name is Isabela de Matos. I am an applied mathematician and computational scientist active in systems' energy consumption estimation and optimisation. I also co-run the Mogrene studio, where I work on building minimal websites and resource-sensitive tools.

This is a personal logbook, where I share some notes on a variety of subjects and projects, including experimental vandwelling and low-resolution photography. This site does not use ads or cookies, it has an open source code, and all of its content is written by a human.
