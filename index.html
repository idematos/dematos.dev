My name is Isabela. I am an applied mathematician and computational scientist active in systems' energy consumption estimation and optimisation. I also co-run the Mogrene studio, which is a small collective building minimal websites and resource-sensitive tools.

This is a logbook where I share some notes on a variety of subjects and personal projects, including experimental vandwelling and low-resolution photography. Here you can also find some of the media I have been consuming lately. This website does not use ads or cookies, it has an open source code, and all of its content is written by a human.
